Pools are a way of distributing your tokens to BSC users who stake PLU in the pool.

Listing on the Exchange

How can I list my token on the exchange?

Anybody can "list" any BEP20 token on Pluto. You don't need to contact us or ask permission. You just need to add liquidity to a liquidity pool - that's it. Traders can then trade your token by entering your token's contract address.

Which liquidity pool should I create?

You can create any pool you like, but we recommend creating an 'ALT - USDT' pool. ALT stands for your token.
